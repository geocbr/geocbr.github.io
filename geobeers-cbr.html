geobeersCBR

geobeersCBR is an irregular, informal meeting of geospatially-oriented humans in and around Canberra, usually at a place that serves beer. It follows the global tradition of geobeers - where people meet and discuss ... anything, with like minded other people.

There are no presentations, set topics, or agenda. Nothing is booked, and you can call a geobeer anytime you feel like it, for any reason.

Anyone can organise a geobeer. There's a mailing list for announcements: geobeerscbr@geocbr.community, which you can subscrbe to here: http://b5.cpcloud.com.au/mailman/listinfo/geobeerscbr_geocbr.community.

You can also use the hashtag #geobeersCBR to rustle up a crowd using social media (usually twitter).
